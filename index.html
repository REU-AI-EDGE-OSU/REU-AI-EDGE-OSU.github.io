Program Introduction

The AI-EDGE REU Program at The Ohio State University offers undergraduate students a unique research experience in artificial intelligence and edge computing. Participants will work on real-world projects under the guidance of leading faculty and researchers at the AI-EDGE Institue, gaining hands-on experience and professional development in the rapidly evolving field of AI.

AI-EDGE Institute is an NSF-funded AI Institute for future edge networks and distributed intelligence. Students joining this program will have opportunities to work with and learn from world class experts in network systems, AI and machine learning (ML), and conduct research projects along a broad range of AI and Networking related topics.
